New York City Dogs

How many dogs in NYC share your dog's name?




Make your dog count!

All dogs in NYC must be licensed. Having a license makes it easier to reunite with your dog if it gets lost and allows your dog to run off-leash in dog runs and other designated areas. The licensing fee also helps support the City’s animal shelters.

Learn more about dog licensing.
